About AdaptiveEd Connect

Adaptive Ed Connect is a revolutionary educational portal designed for children with dyslexia and Down syndrome.

innovative platform not only offers personalized learning resources catering to diverse needs but also serves as a hub connecting parents and NGOs in the special education domain.

Focused on inclusivity, it empowers children with unique abilities, fostering a supportive community.
